Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Yappi ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

Microphone and Audio Data

To provide pet translation features, we require access to your device's microphone. Audio recordings of your pets are processed to generate translations.

  • Audio data is sent to our AI processing providers (Google Gemini) solely for the purpose of translation.
  • We do not permanently store your raw audio recordings on our servers.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our app, including:

  • Device type and operating system.
  • App crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Feature usage statistics (via Amplitude).

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Gemini API: For AI-based audio analysis and translation.
  • Firebase (Google): For analytics, authentication, and database services.
  • Amplitude: For analyzing user behavior and improving app experience.
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions and in-app purchases.

3. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your information and strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect it. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@tolianlabs.com.
